aspiring martian.

I'm obsessed with Mars. Humanity can't outlive the Earth - it's a species imperitive that we get out their among the stars. Mars is a great first step!

I believe that humanity's future is among the stars. I spend a lot of my time volunteering with the Mars Society of Canada as the lead designer of the Mars Explorer Program. The Mars Explorer Program is an open-source, accessible and enjoyable online learning platform designed to inspire students across Canada to explore Mars. The program is set to launch later this year. The program will begin as a collection of teacher resources that can be used in schools across Canada. Eventually, the program will expand beyond teacher resources to include student competitions, educational videos, online courses and after school virtual clubs.

Building Athens

The first education system on Mars

We don't get to pick the second generation of Martians, so an effective education system on Mars is vital to humanity's attempt to become interplanetary. Building Athens is an article series that explores the challenges and potential solutions to educating students on Mars. My first article is part of the Mars Society of Canada's Marslog. The article pays tribute to Athens, the center of education that led to the flourishing of a new civilization and explores the major challenges to building the first interplanetary education system. Hopefull Mars becomes a new center of education leading to the flourishing of yet another amazing civilization.

Part 1: Challenges to Building a Martian Education System

In this inaugural article, I explore the key challenges that need to be overcome in order to establish the first education system on Mars.
